Faux Grant Proposal for Pollinator Workshop (PDF)
This 25 page grant proposal requests funding for workshops in Massachusetts, where residents can learn about bees and craft structures to house solitary pollinators.

Historical Research Paper (PDF)
This is an 11 page research paper that analyzes the role of synthetic fuel in World War II. It narrates the creation of the Bergius process in a booming chemical industry in Germany, and discusses how it influenced the Nazi Party’s decision to wage war.

Literary Analysis Essay (PDF)
In this 7 page essay, I closely analyze Charles Dickens’ The Mystery of Edwin Drood. I compare the antagonist’s desire to commit homicide to the popularity of murder narratives in Victorian England, especially among the working class.

Legal Research Paper (PDF)
For this 5 page research essay, I use Supreme Court and federal rulings to argue whether or not placing hidden microphones in university dorms is constitutional.
